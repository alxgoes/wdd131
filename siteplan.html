Site Plan for Employee Portfolio

Site Name

Company Employee Portfolio

Reason: This name is relevant as it clearly indicates that the site is a portfolio showcasing the skills, projects, and achievements of the employees of a company.

Site Purpose

The site provides a platform to showcase the skills, projects, and achievements of the employees. It serves as a professional portfolio that helps highlight the talents of the team members to potential clients, partners, and new hires.

Scenarios

  • What projects has the employee worked on?
  • How can I contact the employee for potential collaboration?

Color Schema

Headings: Black (#000000)

Backgrounds: Gray (#808080)

Accents: Yellow (#FFD700)

Typography

Headings: 'Pacifico', cursive

Body: 'Roboto', sans-serif
